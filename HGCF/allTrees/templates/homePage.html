About Our Farm

Nestled in the heart of Michigan, Heavens Gates Cherry Farm is a family-run farm dedicated to growing high-quality, organic, hand-tended fruit. We proudly grow sweet cherries and juicy strawberries, perfect for fresh snacking, homemade desserts, and summer memories. Whether you're stopping by for U-pick or placing a local order, we’re here to bring you the freshest fruit straight from our fields.
