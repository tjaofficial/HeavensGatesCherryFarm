From 50 Trees to a Farm Full of Life

Over a decade ago, Heaven’s Gates Cherry Farm began with 50 baby cherry trees and a dream. Through the years, the farm has seen highs and lows, triumphs and hardships, quiet prayers, long days, and more than a few battles with unpredictable Michigan weather.

The Journey

Faith, Frost, Fallen Tunnels, and Fruit

1

It Started With 50 Baby Cherry Trees

Heaven’s Gates Cherry Farm began over a decade ago with 50 baby cherry trees and a dream. It was simple at first: plant something meaningful, work the land, and trust that with time, care, and faith, something beautiful would grow.

2

The Highs, the Lows, and the Late Spring Frost

Like any real farm story, the road was not smooth. Late spring frosts came through and killed buds on the trees before they ever had the chance to become fruit. Some years brought hope. Other years brought hard lessons.

3

High Tunnels Went Up

To protect the trees and push the farm forward, high tunnels were erected. They represented progress, protection, and a new chapter for the farm — proof that even after setbacks, we were still willing to build.

4

Then Michigan Snow Had Other Plans

Then came the kind of Michigan snow that does not ask permission. The weight collapsed the high tunnels and smashed most of the cherry trees. It was one of those moments where you either quit or you dig in deeper.

5

The Farm Kept Growing

Through it all, we kept moving. Apple trees were added. Nectarine trees were added. Thousands of Earliglow strawberry plants joined the farm. A beautiful garden began taking shape. What started as a dream slowly became a place filled with life.

6

Heads High, Prayers Strong, Bodies Working

We kept our heads high, prayers strong, and bodies working. Heaven’s Gates Cherry Farm has blossomed into what it is today because of faith, family, hard work, and the belief that good things can still grow after hard seasons.
